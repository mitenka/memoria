Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Memoria app ("the App") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, and no sign-in is required to play. The App does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, or any other personal information.

Data Storage

Any game data, such as your high score, is stored locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party. Removing the App from your device deletes this local data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children. It is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
